Sesame Chicken

Description

Sesame-flavored chicken thighs are crispy on the outside, yet tender and juicy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

Steps

Step 1:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sesame oil, soy sauce, sriracha sauce, honey, and rice vinegar.
  2. Add the chicken thighs to the bowl and toss to coat them in the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 24 hours for more flavor.

Step 2:

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Remove the chicken thighs from the marinade and place them in a single layer in the air fryer basket.
  3. Cook for 20-25 minutes, flipping the chicken halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and has an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Step 3:

  1. Remove the chicken from the air fryer and let it rest for a few minutes.
  2. Garnish with chopped green onion and toasted sesame seeds before serving.
