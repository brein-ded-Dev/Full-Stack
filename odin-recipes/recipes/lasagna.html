Lasagna

Home

Description

A great lasagna only needs two things: a thick, rich, super-meaty sauce (and lots of it!) and a ricotta filling where only the finest cheeses are welcomed. For the sauce, I love a 50-50 combo of Italian sausage and lean ground beef.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 8 ounce fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Pasta

  • 16 ounce lasagna noodles

Ricotta Filling

  • 15 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 2 large egg
  • 8 ounce fresh mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Cheese Topping

  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup Mozzarella cheese

Steps:

    Step 1: Making the Meat Sauce
  1. Cook and stir ground beef and sausage in a large saucepan over medium heat until browned and crumbly. About 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in mushrooms, salt, black pepper, italian seasoning, and pepper flakes.
  3. Increase heat to medium-high and cook until mushrooms have given off their juices and the bottom of the pan is dry. about 4-6 minutes
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, and basil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer until Meat is extremely tender. About 2 hours.
  6. Add water if sauce become too thick.
  7. Add water if the sauce is too thick. Skim Fat, then season and turn off the heat.
    Step 2: Preparing the Pasta
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
    Step 3: Making the Ricotta Filling
  1. Whisk Eggs in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in Cheese, parsley, salt, and black pepper.
    Step 4: Assembling the Lasagna
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  4. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  5. Tap the dish lightly to settle the layers
  6. Top with a layer of meat sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce and cheese.
    Step 5: Baking the Lasagna
  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  3. Let the lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before serving.
