Waffles

Description

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

Step 1:

  1. Gather all ingredients

Step 2:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.

Step 3:

  1. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  2. Preheat the waffle iron.

Step 4:

  1. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

Step 5:

  1. Cook the waffles according to the waffle iron's instructions.
